---
title: "Right to Informed Legal Counsel"
slug: right-to-counsel
summary: "Require that citizens consult an attorney before waiving the right to legal representation."
status: draft
order: 5
---

No citizen may be proffered, by any government official or agent, to waive the right to an attorney without having first discussed the fullness of the accusations or charges against them with an attorney representing their interests.
