---
title: "Election Holidays"
slug: election-holidays
summary: "Make all election days holidays and protect the right to vote from employer retaliation."
status: draft
order: 4
---

**Section 1.** All State, Local, and Federal elections shall happen on a holiday. If an election is to occur and it is not a holiday, it becomes a holiday for all compensation and wage calculation.

**Section 2.** For the purposes of employment or similar demands, the act of voting, and the time required to perform said act of voting, shall be guaranteed and exercising the right to vote shall be protected from all forms of retaliation.
