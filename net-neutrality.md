---
title: "Net Neutrality"
slug: net-neutrality
summary: "Enshrine equal access to information and prohibit interference with internet traffic."
status: draft
order: 1
---

Access to information generally, and the Internet in particular, being necessary for the protection of free expression, free thought, and free association; no corporation, person, government, or other entity shall, in any manner, throttle, filter, shape, or in any other way interfere with or alter information to or from any destination or source. With the reasonable exceptions of protecting hosts and services, with which one has a significant interest, from malicious attack or similar interference.
